About

Welcome to GK Consulting, where innovation meets reliability in the world of Terraform and DevOps. Founded by two visionary friends with a solid grounding in Platform Engineering and Backend Design, our journey began from a shared frustration — a frustration with the prevalent bugs and the often overlooked missing features in Terraform providers. It seemed to us they were an afterthought, a sentiment we knew many shared in the tech community. But rather than accept the status quo, we saw an opportunity to contribute, to improve, and to lead.

At the heart of our mission lies a simple yet profound desire: to refine and simplify the tech stack. We believe in a tech ecosystem that is simple and efficient, no matter the project or partner we are working with. This guiding principle has led us across a diverse landscape of challenges and successes, from lending our expertise to Fortune 500 giants like HPE, PG&E, to fueling innovation within dynamic startups such as AMP Robotics, TIFIN-AMP, and RVShare. Our journey has been one of versatility, innovation, and relentless pursuit of excellence.

Our commitment extends beyond the confines of contract work; it reaches into the very core of the open-source community. We are not just participants but active contributors to the projects we hold dear. Open source is not just a principle for us; it’s a practice — a way to give back, share knowledge, and foster a community of like-minded individuals driven by the same goal: to make technology better, more accessible, and more reliable for everyone.
